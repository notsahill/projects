things it does

small cat, big personality

👀

Follows your cursor

Its eyes track your mouse. Flick fast and it scampers after the pointer.

⌨️

Types with you

Taps two little keys as you type. Go too fast and it overheats — steam and all.

🧘

Stretch & water

Walks to center, does a stretch or laps from a bowl, then trots back. Gentle nudges.

🍅

Pomodoro

A live countdown floats over its head while you focus. Tomato for work, coffee for breaks.

✅

Cheers your AI agent

Jumps with “task done” when Cursor, Claude Code or Antigravity finishes a response.

🎨

Make it yours

Pick the coat color and markings — tabby, tuxedo, calico, mittens. Pin it in place too.

two minutes

getting it home

1
Open the .dmg and drag DeskPet into Applications.
2
Unsigned build — clear the quarantine flag once in Terminal:
xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/DeskPet.app
…then double-click. (Or right-click → Open if you only see “unidentified developer”.)
3
Lives in the menubar 🐾. For typing reactions: System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility → enable DeskPet → relaunch.
